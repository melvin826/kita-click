PadalaX — Crowd-Based Delivery Network

A flexible delivery program that lets members earn by delivering parcels within the KitaClick network.

How it Works

  1. Members sign up as PadalaX partners via the app.
  2. AI matches deliveries to travellers and local couriers.
  3. Blockchain-based tracking and insurance per parcel.
  4. Payments settled via KitaCrypto Wallet.

Earnings

Typical earning per delivery: ₱50–₱200; high-value routes can earn more. KitaClick charges a platform fee on delivery fares; partners keep the remainder.

KitaCrypto Wallet

All commissions, bonuses and refunds are credited to members' KitaCrypto Wallet. Wallet supports balance view, withdrawals (local payout integrations), and merchant discounts.

Wallet Features

  • Instant credits for verified transactions
  • Peer-to-peer transfers
  • Merchant & partner discounts

Security

Multi-factor authentication and rate-limited payout endpoints. For production, use KYC for large withdrawals and integrate anti-fraud monitoring.
